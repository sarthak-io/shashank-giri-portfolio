Challenge

Alippo, an emerging ed-tech platform, was facing high customer acquisition costs and struggling with customer retention in a competitive market. They needed to optimize their marketing spend while improving user engagement and retention metrics.

Solution

We implemented a comprehensive strategy to address both acquisition and retention:

  • Conducted in-depth analysis of customer acquisition channels to identify inefficiencies
  • Redesigned the marketing funnel with targeted messaging for different customer segments
  • Implemented innovative digital marketing strategies focused on high-intent audiences
  • Developed a customer retention program with personalized engagement touchpoints
  • Created automated win-back campaigns for at-risk customers

Results

The strategy delivered significant improvements:

  • Reduced Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by 38% through channel optimization
  • Increased customer retention from 20% to 28%, an 8 percentage point improvement
  • Improved conversion rates by 42% through optimized landing pages and user journeys
  • Achieved 3.2x ROI on marketing spend within 6 months
  • Established Alippo as a fast-growing player in the ed-tech space

Approach & Methodology

Our approach combined analytics with creative marketing solutions:

  • Data-Driven Channel Optimization: Used attribution modeling to identify the most efficient acquisition channels
  • Behavioral Analysis: Analyzed user behavior patterns to identify retention triggers and churn signals
  • Targeted Digital Initiatives: Developed highly targeted campaigns for specific customer segments
  • Continuous Testing: Implemented A/B testing framework for all marketing assets and user journeys
