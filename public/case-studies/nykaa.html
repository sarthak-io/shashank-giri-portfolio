Challenge

Nykaa, India's leading beauty and personal care e-commerce platform, faced challenges in optimizing their substantial marketing budget and improving customer retention in a highly competitive market with increasing customer acquisition costs.

Solution

We implemented a comprehensive marketing and retention strategy:

  • Managed ₹1.3 crore monthly paid media budgets across Google, Meta, and programmatic channels
  • Developed advanced CRM segmentation models to identify high-value customer cohorts
  • Implemented predictive modeling to forecast customer behavior and optimize retention efforts
  • Created personalized marketing campaigns based on purchase history and browsing behavior
  • Established cross-channel attribution models to optimize marketing spend allocation

Results

The strategy delivered significant improvements:

  • Consistently achieved 4.5-5x Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) across channels
  • Improved customer retention rates by implementing targeted win-back campaigns
  • Increased average order value by 22% through personalized recommendations
  • Reduced customer acquisition costs by 18% through optimized channel mix
  • Successfully scaled marketing efforts across a 1.5 crore user base

Approach & Methodology

Our approach combined data science with marketing expertise:

  • Advanced Analytics: Utilized machine learning models to segment customers and predict behavior
  • Multi-channel Strategy: Coordinated messaging across paid, owned, and earned media
  • Continuous Experimentation: Implemented A/B testing framework for all marketing initiatives
  • Customer Lifecycle Management: Developed targeted strategies for each stage of the customer journey
