Challenge

Asian Paints, India's leading paint company, needed to modernize their digital presence and optimize lead generation in a highly competitive market. The company was struggling with low conversion rates and inefficient digital marketing spend.

Solution

We implemented a comprehensive digital transformation strategy:

  • Executed an extensive rebranding campaign to strengthen market position
  • Implemented Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP) to improve mobile user experience
  • Developed a rigorous A/B testing framework for all digital assets
  • Optimized lead generation funnels with personalized user journeys
  • Managed a ₹2 crore monthly digital budget with data-driven allocation

Results

The transformation yielded exceptional results:

  • Increased monthly appointments from 200 to 1,000 (5x growth)
  • Achieved 5-6x Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) consistently
  • Reduced customer acquisition costs by 32%
  • Won MarTech Award for innovative digital marketing approach
  • Established Asian Paints as a digital leader in the home improvement sector

Approach & Methodology

Our approach combined strategic vision with tactical execution:

  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Implemented advanced analytics to guide all marketing decisions
  • Continuous Optimization: Established weekly optimization cycles for all campaigns
  • Cross-Channel Integration: Created seamless experiences across digital touchpoints
  • AI-Powered Personalization: Leveraged machine learning for customer segmentation and targeting
